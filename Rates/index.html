I'm arriving via Cruise Ship. Can I book a day trip?

Absolutely. We specialize in "Dock-to-Dock" service for cruise passengers.

We will arrange pickup directly from the port (Coxen Hole or Mahogany Bay), maximize your time on the water, and guarantee a timely return well before your ship departs.

Check Port Availability
Are dates fixed, or can we build a custom trip?

While we offer standard packages, we are fully flexible. Whether you need a 3-day weekend or a 10-day expedition, we customize the itinerary to fit your group size and schedule.

We can also accommodate split groups where some guests fish and others relax at the lodge.

What exactly is included in the Lodging Packages?

Our packages are designed to be hassle-free. They include:

  • Private Airport/Ferry Transfers
  • Private Chef (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)
  • Luxury Lodging & Starlink Wi-Fi
  • Daily Daily Boat & Guide Fees

*Alcohol and specialty meal requests can be arranged for an additional cost.

Do you offer eco-tours or non-fishing activities?

Yes. The East End is beautiful and we encourage you to explore it. For an additional fee, we can arrange:

Mangrove Tunnel Tours Shark Diving Snorkeling Island Hopping
Do I need to bring my own gear?

We recommend anglers bring their own setups (8wt-10wt) to be most comfortable. However, we do have high-quality rods and reels available for rent if you prefer to travel light. Please mention this when booking.
