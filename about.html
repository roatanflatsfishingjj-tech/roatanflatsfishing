Our Story

Born on the Water

More than just fishing.
It’s a Roatan Legacy.

Welcome to Roatan Flats Fishing JJ. We are not just a charter service; we are a family of anglers born and raised in the mangroves and flats of Islas de la Bahía.

We know where the Permit tail on the incoming tide, where the Bonefish school in the shadows, and where the resident Tarpon roll at sunrise. When you fish with JJ, you aren't just booking a boat; you are unlocking decades of local knowledge.

"The difference between a good day and a legendary day is knowing the water like the back of your hand."

Your Guide

Meet Captain Joevy

With over 10 years navigating the East End flats, Captain Joevy specializes in the Grand Slam. His patience and eagle eyes are your best tool on the water.

Whether you are looking to land your first Bonefish or hunting for a trophy Tarpon, the Captain knows exactly how to position the boat against the wind and tide to give you the perfect shot.
