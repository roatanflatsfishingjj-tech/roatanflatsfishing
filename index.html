Grand Slam

These waters demand precision. We target the three most coveted species in saltwater fly fishing.

01.

Bonefish

Albula vulpes

The Ghost of the Flats. Speed is their defense. Found in shallow schools or cruising singly.

Rod: 7-8wt
Leader: 10-12lb
Diff: Medium
02.

Permit

Trachinotus falcatus

The ultimate challenge. Notoriously picky and visually demanding. The holy grail of the fly.

Rod: 9-10wt
Leader: 16-20lb
Diff: Expert
03.

Tarpon

Megalops atlanticus

The Silver King. Aerial acrobatics and raw power. They will test your knots and your nerves.

Rod: 10-12wt
Shock: 60lb
Diff: High
