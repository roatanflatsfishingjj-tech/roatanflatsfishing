Grand Slam

Three species, three completely different tests. This section now lands faster visually and feels more modern without getting flashy.

01.

Bonefish

Albula vulpes

Fast, clean, and unforgiving. Bonefish reward quick reads, quiet shots, and good angles.

Rod: 7-8wt
Leader: 10-12lb
Diff: Medium
02.

Permit

Trachinotus falcatus

The fish guests dream about. Permit demand patience, accuracy, and nerves that stay steady.

Rod: 9-10wt
Leader: 16-20lb
Diff: Expert
03.

Tarpon

Megalops atlanticus

Heavy pulls, head shakes, and chaos in the best way. Tarpon bring the power piece of the story.

Rod: 10-12wt
Shock: 60lb
Diff: High
